Saraswati Puja: A Celebration of Knowledge, Art, and Learning

We are delighted to announce the celebration of Saraswati Puja, dedicated to Goddess Saraswati, the embodiment of wisdom, learning, and the arts. This festival, traditionally celebrated with devotion in Bengal, is an occasion where knowledge meets culture, and everyone is invited to be part of this joyous event. Saraswati Puja is a day to honor the goddess who inspires education, creativity, and music. The idol of Goddess Saraswati is adorned in white, symbolizing purity and serenity, and offerings include books, musical instruments, and pens, as a mark of respect for the goddessâ€™s blessings in these domains.

What to Expect

  • Traditional Puja
    Experience the rituals, mantras, and prayers that seek the blessings of Goddess Saraswati for knowledge and enlightenment.
  • Cultural Performances
    Enjoy a blend of music, dance, and recitations celebrating the essence of art and learning.
  • Community Gathering
    Connect with people from diverse backgrounds, as the event is open to everyone, irrespective of faith or culture.
  • Childrens' Participation
    This is an excellent opportunity for young learners to engage in a cultural experience that celebrates the importance of education and creativity.

An Open Invitation

We warmly invite you to join us for Saraswati Puja. This is not just a religious celebration but also a cultural festival that celebrates inclusivity and shared values. Whether you want to offer your prayers, enjoy the cultural programs, or simply learn about a new tradition, this event is for you. Let's come together to honor the goddess of learning and celebrate the universal values of knowledge and creativity. We look forward to welcoming you all!

We welcome you not just as guests but as fellow travellers on the journey of celebrating the sacred ties that bind land, community, and spirit.

** Note: 🙏 Attendance is restricted to Bandhan members and pre-acknowledged guests only. 🙏
